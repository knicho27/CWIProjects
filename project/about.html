Pam's passion and curiosity for painting have won her numerous awards at the best shows and exhibits in Colorado. She is inspired by the movement and rhythm of patterns, bold, pleasing, vibrant colors, masterful contrasts and unpredictable asymmetry balanced by strategically placed lines. She believes art is an expression of creativity that should bring emotion to the mind, interest to the eye, engages the viewer and should stand strongly and independently on it's own requiring no “artsy” explanation. She has her own very distinct style…a blend of technical, geometric precision with contemporary, lively colors!
